Smart Locks have historically been an expensive technology. With advancements in technology and understanding real consumer pain points, we have introduced Native Smart Door Lock to make life more convenient and secure:

Among the myriad of smart devices that promise to enhance our daily lives, Native Smart Door Lock stands out as a game-changer in convenience and home security. If you find yourself pondering whether to make the switch from traditional locks, this article shares compelling reasons:

Convenience of not managing keys

Imagine a world where you no longer fumble for keys in your bag or worry about forgetting them altogether. With a smart lock, your unique fingerprint becomes your key granting you hassle-free access to your home. You don’t have to ever again worry if somebody will duplicate your key. Additionally, alternative methods of entry such as passcodes and IC cards provide a level of flexibility for elderly and young children who might have faded fingerprints.

Sharing home access from anywhere

One of the standout features of Native Smart Door Lock is the ability to control and monitor access to your home remotely. Whether you’re at work, on vacation, or simply away for the day, smart locks empower you to grant access to guests, service providers, or family members with just a few taps on your smartphone. You can generate and share a one-time password (OTP) or approve/ reject visitor requests from anywhere. 

Managing your rental property

Native Smart Door Lock gives you the power to customize access permissions for different individuals. Whether it’s granting temporary access to guests, providing permanent access to family members or sharing access remotely with your property broker/ caretaker. Smart locks offer a level of flexibility that traditional locks simply cannot match. Also, you don’t have to worry if a tenant loses or misplaces the key. Installing smart locks not only enhances the security and convenience of your home but can also contribute to its overall market appeal.

Monitoring alerts remotely

Native Smart Door Lock offers activity monitoring, allowing you to track who enters and exits your home and when. This not only enhances security but also provides valuable insights into the comings and goings in your household, especially if your elderly parents stay far off. Also, you receive real-time alerts about incorrect attempts to unlock or break the lock.

Child lock. Privacy latch and more

Native Smart Door Lock comes with thoughtful features such as child lock to restrict children from stepping out accidentally. Also, the privacy latch enables folks to disable the smart unlocking ensuring no surprise entries. Also, you can manage users on your App and receive alerts when the battery is low. 

In summary – while the initial cost of smart locks may be a consideration, the advantages in terms of convenience, and security make them a worthy addition to any home
